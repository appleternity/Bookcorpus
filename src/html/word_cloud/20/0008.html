Instruction

A word-cloud representation is a group of words that we generated to characterize a story snippet. For example, a "word-cloud representation of nouns" is a group of nouns that might occur, or are semantically relevant, in a story snippet. Notice that the words in the word-cloud shows the concept of the story so the words will not necessarily appear in the story.
