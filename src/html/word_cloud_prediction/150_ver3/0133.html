Immediately, he heard an alarm vibrating through the ship, warning its occupants of the impact.
Despite the fact most of the sophisticated skills of his armor had been switched off, at least it had protected him from the fall.
His head wasn't spinning.

She possessed the kind of technology that could hack through a Xantos Guard's armor.

She was an assassin.
She had to be.

Andalusia was in danger.
