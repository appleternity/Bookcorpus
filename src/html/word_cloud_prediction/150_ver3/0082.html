She needed help...
Amon slithered over to the fallen gun, picked it up, and inspected it curiously.
"I've never seen a gun like this before.
What is it called?"
"It's called," Hexa took a deep breath.
"Fuck you!"
Amon's eyes flashed with anger and he gave her a little shake.
"Tell me, female, or I'll crush all the bones in your legs!"
Hexa's eyes widened in fear at his threat.
She knew he'd follow through with it too.
"It's...a blaster.
Well, Jax called it a blaster - he made it."
"I see."
Amon crushed the gun into a ball in his bare hand and made sure that Hexa was watching him.
"No!"
Hexa cried out.
Amon removed the other gun from Hexa's holster next.
"And this one I remember...what was it called again?"
"A sonic shotgun," Hexa snapped.
"Another weapon far too dangerous to be left in the hands of the humans."
Amon crushed it to pieces next.
"No," Hexa murmured sadly as she watched the pieces of metal fall and hit the floor.
Her gray eyes teared up, but she wouldn't let her tears fall.
She'd failed...failed to avenge Jax.
And now she needed rescuing.
I'm so pathetic.
But even if I send an SOS no one would be able to get here in time to save me.
I'm so dead.
Unless that someone could teleport, a voice piped up in Hexa's mind.
That's right!
Malakye Sterling could teleport.
She hated asking him for help, but she had no choice.
As stealthily as she could Hexa got out her smart phone and began to text Malakye behind her back.
She needed to buy herself some more time...
"Amon, there's something that's been bothering me all this time.
Why didn't you kill me that day...you killed everyone else.
Did you leave me alive to suffer?"
Hexa questioned with a tremor to her voice.
Amon arched a brow at her.
"I didn't spare you.
By the time I was battling you I'd used up most of my venom on the others in your squad.
My venom killed them, and I injected what I could into your legs, but obviously it didn't kill you.
Somehow, you managed to survive and escape the temple without my noticing.
And your legs...were paralyzed as a consequence of having that much venom pumped into them apparently."
Hexa's eyes widened with this startling revelation.
All this time I thought Amon left me alive so that I could suffer over the loss of my brother.
But it was just an accident.
This meant that in a way her squad had truly protected her life with theirs.
Their sacrifice hadn't been in vain. Guys...Jax...thank you.
"And now I will rectify my mistake and kill you...slowly for your defiance, human female."
A sinister smile curled Amon's lips.
No!
Hexa mentally shouted.
Amon began to constrict his tail around her legs and Hexa let out a scream of terror.
She could feel her bones breaking!
At that moment, Malakye materialized inside of the restaurant and looked around.
He swiftly spotted Hexa in Amon's clutches.
"Hexa!"
He raised his hand and called out, "Time Stop!"
A blue stone pendant glowed brightly on Amon's chest, and nothing happened.
"What the hell?"
Malakye muttered darkly to himself as his brow furrowed in confusion.
Amon turned his golden gaze to Malakye.
"Did you just try to use your infamous 'Time Stop' on me?
Well, it's a good thing I decided to team up with the Lunarians.
Garnetta gave me this pendant yesterday at the club Iron Casket as a gift of thanks for my loyalty to their cause."
The demon fingered the pendant thoughtfully.
"So that's why you were there at the club yesterday," Hexa said through gritted teeth.
"You were forging an alliance with the Lunarians.
Dammit..."
"Amon, let Hexa go!"
Malakye demanded sternly.
He didn't like the sight of blood on Hexa's leg braces one bit.
"You're going to have to make me, Malakye Sterling," Amon challenged.
"Well, I plan to."
Malakye whipped out his wooden sword hilt and willed a blade of psychic energy to form.
He pointed his glowing blue sword at Amon threateningly.
Amon reached up and undid his turban to reveal his head of live, sandy-colored cobras before tossing his turban to the floor.
Malakye charged towards Amon, and the snake demon slithered across the floor towards the alien with incredible, superhuman speed.
Amon lowered his head and willed his cobras to attack Malakye simultaneously.
"Bite!"
Malakye dodged spitting cobras and swung his sword.
He ended up chopping off a few of the cobras' heads.
Amon let out a roar of outrage in response.
"How dare you...!"
The demon regretted that he didn't have his usual scimitar with him.
Amon let out a frustrated sound before tossing Hexa aside.
She went flying and hit the floor hard and rolled across it.
Hexa let out a yelp of pain.
"Hexa!"
Malakye called out in concern.
He turned to glare at Amon, his emerald green eyes flashing with rage.
"You bastard!"
He attacked again.
However, now that Amon had his tail free he began to put it to good use.
Amon sent his tail flying towards Malakye who was forced to jump up to avoid the deadly tail swipe.
Then Amon brought his tail up and then down, intending to simply smush Malakye like a pancake.
Malakye used all of his skill and reflexes to dodge out of the way.
Amon was fast.
And Malakye was quickly tiring.
The battle against Garnetta had taken a lot out of him and he hadn't had the time to soak up some moonlight.
This meant he was running low on psychic energy.
The wounds on his back had also barely had time to heal and he could feel the wounds opening up again.
Amon attacked Malakye with his snakes again and the alien sliced off another cobra head.
Amon snarled, and whipped his tail out at Malakye, finally managing to trip the plant alien.
Oh shit.
Malakye tried to scramble out of the way, but it was too late.
Amon coiled his tail around the alien's legs and began to drag him across the floor.
The tail began to coil around Malakye's torso and kept his arms pinned to his sides so that he was unable to use his sword.
Once Malakye was in front of Amon, the demon flipped the alien onto his back.
"And now you die, Malakye Sterling."
The demon reared up over Malakye, and his snakes hissed in Malakye's face in unison.
"No, Mal..."
Hexa murmured as she watched what was happening from her place on the floor.
The snakes reared and prepared to strike.
Suddenly, the door to the Chinese Food Restaurant was kicked in and Agent Darkhart strolled inside with an air of authority, his long black trench coat billowing out behind him.
"Well, what do we have here?"
Agent Darkhart started in his gravely voice as he took in the scene before him.
His eyes narrowed into slits.
"Let Malakye Sterling go, Demon Commander Amon."
At that moment several super soldiers began to pour into the restaurant.
Amon could sense these men were not fully human and so posed a threat.
"Fuck.
I don't like these odds."
Amon hissed in Malakye's face.
"You got lucky, boy."
The snake demon released the alien and shot off, slithering away in the direction of the kitchen.
"After him," Agent Darkhart directed his men with a wave of his hand.
"Yes, Sir!"
the soldiers yelled out before pursuing Amon, guns raised.
Malakye quickly got to his feet and made his way over to Hexa.
He crouched at her side and frowned when he saw the horrible state she was in.
She was fading in and out of consciousness due to the excruciating pain she was in.
Her legs...were a mess.
Malakye swooped Hexa up into his arms and cradled her against his chest.
Agent Darkhart approached them with swift strides.
Hexa struggled to open her eyes and glanced at Agent Darkhart.
"Agent Darkhart, you came to rescue me?
How did you even know where I was?"
A slight smirk formed on the agent's thin lips.
"I went to your room to speak with you and saw what was on your computer monitors.
I watched the footage of Amon at Iron Casket that you'd retrieved.
I then used your facial recognition software to track him down...and you."
He reached out and patted her arm.
"I'm glad we made it in time, Hexa."
Wonder flashed across Hexa's face.
"Aren't you angry with me?
I broke the rules?
Aren't you going to punish me?"
Agent Darkhart looked at Hexa's bloody legs and frowned.
